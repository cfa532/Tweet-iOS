Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new post?

Tap the compose button (+) in the bottom navigation to create a new post. You can add text, photos, and videos to share with your followers.

How do I follow other users?

Use the search tab to find users and tap the follow button on their profile. You'll then see their posts in your feed.

Can I edit my posts after publishing?

Currently, posts cannot be edited after publishing. Please review your content carefully before posting.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Tap the three dots menu on any post and select "Report" to flag inappropriate content for review.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Your data is encrypted and we never share your personal information with third parties.

How do I change my profile picture?

Go to your profile, tap on your current profile picture, and select a new image from your photo library.

Can I use M2O offline?

M2O requires an internet connection to load content and post updates. However, you can view previously loaded content when offline.

Getting Started

  1. Download and install the M2O app from the App Store
  2. Create an account or sign in with your existing credentials
  3. Complete your profile with a photo and bio to personalize your account
  4. Start following other users to see their content in your feed
  5. Create your first post and start sharing with the community!

Troubleshooting

App won't load or crashes

  • Check your internet connection
  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • Restart your device
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store
  • Clear app cache by deleting and reinstalling the app

Can't upload photos or videos

  • Check that you have granted camera and photo library permissions
  • Ensure you have sufficient storage space on your device
  • Try uploading smaller files (under 100MB)
  • Check your internet connection speed

Notifications not working

  • Go to Settings > Notifications > M2O and ensure notifications are enabled
  • Check that you're signed in to the app
  • Verify your device's notification settings
  • Try toggling notifications off and on

Can't find specific content

  • Use the search function to look for specific users or topics
  • Check if the content has been deleted or made private
  • Refresh your feed by pulling down
  • Try logging out and back in
