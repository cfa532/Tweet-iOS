Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new tweet?

Tap the compose button (usually a + or pen icon) in the bottom navigation or top right corner of the app. Write your message and tap "Tweet" to post.

How do I follow other users?

Visit a user's profile by tapping on their username or profile picture, then tap the "Follow" button to start following their updates.

How do I like or retweet a post?

Below each tweet, you'll find action buttons. Tap the heart icon to like, or the retweet icon to share the post with your followers.

How do I send a direct message?

Navigate to the Messages section, tap the compose button, select a user, and start typing your message. Tap send when ready.

How do I bookmark a tweet?

Tap the bookmark icon (bookmark symbol) below any tweet to save it to your bookmarks for later viewing.

How do I search for content or users?

Use the search bar at the top of the app to find tweets, users, or hashtags. You can also use advanced search filters for more specific results.

How do I change my profile picture?

Go to your profile, tap "Edit Profile," then tap on your current profile picture to select a new image from your photo library.

How do I enable notifications?

Go to your device Settings > Notifications > Tweet iOS, and enable notifications for the app. You can customize which types of notifications you receive.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Tap the three dots menu on any tweet, then select "Report" and choose the reason for reporting the content.

How do I block or mute a user?

Visit the user's profile, tap the three dots menu, and select either "Block" or "Mute" depending on your preference.
